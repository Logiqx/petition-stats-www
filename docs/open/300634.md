# Stop Forestry England granting licenses for Fox & Hare hunts

### Background

For the most recent hunting season, Forestry England gave hunting licences for 33 fox & hare “trail hunts”. Despite the hunting of wild mammals with dogs being illegal, two of the licensed trail hunts have received, or been associated with, convictions under the Hunting and Animal Welfare Acts.
