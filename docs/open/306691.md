# Extend maternity leave by 3 months with pay in light of COVID-19

### Background

In light of the recent outbreak and lockdow, those on maternity leave should be given 3 extra months paid leave, at least. This time is for bonding and social engaging with other patents and babies through baby groups which are vital for development and now everything has been cancelled.
